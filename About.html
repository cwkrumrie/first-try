A Bit About Me

I began my coding experience back in the mid-2000s where I customized my first bits of HTML on MySpace. Over time, I've developed a passion for planning, designing, and evaluating digital experiences that are accessible, attractive, and usable.

While my educational background lies in the humanities (I did both my undergraduate and graduate studies in English literature), my experience working in learner-focused positions for more than a decade has allowed me the freedom to explore my interest in web development. As an educator, I have built custom classroom websites and interactive learning experiences.

When I'm not working, I'm usually either reading, playing video games, or relaxing with my husband and our dogs.
