Project #2 - Classroom Website

For this project, I was approached by a colleague who was teaching a literature course using Canvas for the first time. They requested a homepage with accessible links to other Canvas pages, course information, and links to appropriate external resources. During an initial meeting, they also indicated they wanted specific colors and that they wanted a simple, minimalist design.

Target Audience: Community College Instructor / Students
Languages Used: HTML5, CSS
Additional Technologies Used: Canvas LMS, Adobe InDesign
Date Completed: 12/08/2020
